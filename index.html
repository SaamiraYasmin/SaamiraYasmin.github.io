About Saamira

I am Saamira Yasmin, a Data Scientist passionate about leveraging technology to solve real world problems and transforming raw data into impactful solutions. Currently, I work as a Senior Manager, Data Analyst at DTDC Express Limited, where I utilise Machine Learning and Geospatial Analysis to streamline the logistics operations and serve the business requirements. My interest and passion for coding and solving problems began right from my early schooling days. The computer lab used to be one of my favourite places where I would love to program and brainstorm. I pursued my undergraduate degree in Bachelor of Technology from IIT Kharagpur, where I developed a strong foundation in Computer Science and Engineering.

I’m especially interested in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, with a vision to use these tools to drive advancements in sectors like logistics, healthcare, and urban planning. Outside of work, I enjoy playing basketball, listening to music and exploring different cuisines.

To discuss about any relevant oppurtunities or potential collaborations, you can reach me at saamira.y@gmail.com
